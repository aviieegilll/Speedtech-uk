Home›Accreditations

Certified Excellence
In Every Mile

Industry-leading certifications and accreditations ensuring the highest standards.

At Speed-Tech UK, we understand that trust and reliability are paramount. Our services are not just about transportation; they are about ensuring your cargo's safety and compliance at every step.

These accreditations serve as the bedrock of our operations, guaranteeing that our services are underpinned by industry-leading standards and best practices.

ISO 9001:2015

Quality Management

ISO 9001:2015 forms the backbone of Speed-Tech's commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. It establishes the requirements for our Quality Management System (QMS), allowing us to meticulously manage our processes and providing a framework for continuous improvement.

CAA Regulated Agent

GB/RA/00972-01

Speed-Tech UK is a CAA Regulated Agent [GB/RA/00972-01]. Our Regulated Agent status signifies that we provide security controls required by the CAA for air cargo. When you entrust Speed-Tech UK with your cargo, you're choosing the highest level of security measures in place.

ADR Compliance

Dangerous Goods

The International Carriage of Dangerous Goods by Road is governed by the ADR. At Speed-Tech UK, we have our own certified Dangerous Goods Safety Advisers, ensuring our operations align with the harmonised classifications established by the United Nations.

GDP Compliance

Pharmaceutical Logistics

Good Distribution Practice (GDP) mandates that pharmaceuticals are sourced exclusively from licensed supply chains. Our GDP compliance ensures all pharmaceutical cargo is handled and transported in accordance with the strictest industry standards, maintaining product integrity throughout the cold chain.

Aviation Security

Full Compliance

Aviation security is at the heart of everything we do. As a CAA Regulated Agent, we operate under the strictest aviation security protocols, ensuring all export consignments travel within the aviation security corridor with electronic seal systems and GPS tracking.

UKAS Certification

Quality Assurance

Our UKAS (United Kingdom Accreditation Service) certification demonstrates our commitment to operating to the highest quality standards. This internationally recognised accreditation provides independent verification that our quality management systems meet the most demanding requirements.
